    Bromo Tour

    Day 1: Airport/Train station-Handcraft centre Tangulangin Sidoarjo

    Arrived at Juanda airport / Surabaya train station, after meet and greet travellers will be fetched by guide and imediately brought to the centre of handcraft for shoes and bags at Tangulangin, Sidoarjo. After shopping will continue to visit Bromo, luch will be at Rawon nguling restaurant at Probolinggo, will stop for a while at Sukapura to exchange vehicle JEEP (mini 4WD) // HARDTOP. Travellers will stay at Lava View Lodge Bromo Hotel, after check in will continue with free activity.

    Day 2: Bromo Sunrise - Savanah - Rafting at Pekalen river - Airport

    Participants will be sent towards climbing the hills on a JEEP / Hardtop to see the beauty of sunrise and panoramic plateau Tengger views. Then sand down across the ocean to see Bromo crater closer by crossing 245 steps. Further will go towards a green hilly area which looks green throughout the year, ordinary people call "Hill Teletubbies" perhaps because it resembles a picture landscapenya the green hills where Teletubbies play in the movie series. Back to the hotel at 8.00 am for breakfast and relax for a moment before travel and continued toward areas Rafting (whitewater rafting) NOAR'S, Pekalen River - Probolinggo. Arriving at River Pekalen (Basecamp NOAR'S rafting), previous participants get instruction from instructor Rafting NOAR'S, on which procedures to do white water rafting and more. Rafting will commence around 3-4 hours. Finish Rafting (whitewater rafting), participants return to Basecamp NOAR'S to enjoy Lunch meal. Towards the evening will return back Surabaya arriving at night and directly go to hotels, then check-in and rest.

    Day 3: Surabaya

    Free events after brunch at the Hotel, afterwards Guide will fetch you to drive you to Air Juanda Airport, Sidoarjo / / Railway Station Surabaya (Gubeng / Pasar Turi), to return to Your Hometown.
